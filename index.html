About this Place

Bhadrachalam is a sacred town in Telangana, India, where you can immerse yourself in the ancient legends of Ramayana. Visit the majestic Sri Sita Rama Temple, where Lord Rama and his consort Sita are worshipped with devotion and splendor. Witness the colorful celebrations of Sri Rama Navami and Vaikunta Ekadasi, when thousands of pilgrims flock to the temple. Explore the nearby attractions of Parnasala, where Rama spent his exile, and Dummugudem, where he fought the demons. Enjoy a scenic steamboat ride on the River Godavari, surrounded by lush hills and waterfalls. Bhadrachalam is a place where you can experience the divine grace and glory of Rama.
